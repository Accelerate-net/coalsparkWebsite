Our story

Zaitoon is a perfect place in town to taste the best Arabic Food and BBQ, established as one of the most popular entrant into the string which serves Middle-East food along with Indian and Chinese. Authentic Arabian and Indian cuisines made with passion and attention to detail treat your taste buds well.

We serves a variety of delicious cuisines ranging from North Indian, Chinese, Seafood, and Arabian, which are of great taste and quality. The various ingredients that they use are fresh and instantly give a soul-satisfying taste to the diners. The casual decor of the restaurant is vibrant and beautiful. The pocket-friendly menu has been designed with utmost care to make sure that the flavor and spirit of the cuisines come in every dish they serve and it encompasses Barbeque dishes as well. We are extremely proud of our Arabic cuisine and it's undoubtedly the must try at Zaitoon!

With a high degree of commitment and quality, our restaurant chain has expanded gloriously with seven branches in Chennai, two in Bangalore, one in Madurai and two in Qatar. Without compromising on quality and service, we take every step to make your dining experience, a pleasurable one. Very keen in maintaining the food quality and taste, we also desire Zaitoon to be preferred for its refreshing ambience to dine in even for an impromptu lunch or dinner. Come celebrate your special occasions at Zaitoon's Khayal Banquet Hall, we cater to all events.
